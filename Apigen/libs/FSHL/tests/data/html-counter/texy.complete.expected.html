1: Texy! is sexy!
  2: **************
  3: 
  4: Texy! is text-to-HTML formatter and converter library. It allows you to write
  5: structured documents without knowledge or using of HTML language. You write
  6: documents in humane easy-to-read plain text format and Texy! converts it to
  7: structurally and valid (X)HTML code.
  8: 
  9: Texy! is one of the **most complex** formatters. Its possibilities covers images,
 10: links (anchors), nested lists, tables and has full support for CSS((Cascade Style Sheet)).
 11:

113: Typography
114: ==========
115: 
116: - very long words division (with respect for language rules). Example: antidisestablishmentarianism
117: - clickable emails and URL www.davidgrudl.com, david@grudl.com (emails are obfuscated against spambots)
118: - "national" 'typographic' quotes
119: - divider vs. dash: 10-15 vs. north-west
120: - en-dash: one -- two
121: - dimension sign 10 x 20
122: - nonbreakable spaces in phone numbers +420 776 552 046
123: - acronym NATO((North Atlantic Treaty Organisation))
124: - abbr "et al."((and others))
125: - quickCorrects like this(TM) this(R) or (C)this
126: - arrows <-- and --> and <--> ;
127: - ellipsis...
128: - preserve HTML entities
129: - and many others :-)
